Research Interests

During my PhD, my research interests were in scalable Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) algorithms. A common problem when fitting complex models is overfitting, and Bayesian methods provide a natural solution to this. But MCMC, one of the most popular methods for training Bayesian methods, does not scale well with the dataset size. As dataset sizes have been increasing, the need to improve the scalability of MCMC has become clear.
